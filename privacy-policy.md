# Privacy Policy — Numly

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Numly ("the app") is a free iOS calculator app with AI capabilities. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: basically nothing) and how we handle it.

## Data We Collect

**Numly does not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

Specifically, we do not collect:
- Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Your location
- Your device information
- Your usage data or analytics
- Your calculation history (it stays on your device only)

## Microphone & Voice Input

Numly may request access to your microphone to enable voice input for math calculations. This means:
- Voice audio is processed **locally on your device** via Apple's Speech Recognition framework
- Audio is **never recorded or stored** by Numly
- Audio is **never sent** to Numly's servers (we don't have any)
- Microphone access is **optional** — the app works fully without it
- You can revoke microphone access anytime in iOS Settings → Numly

## Speech Recognition

Numly uses Apple's built-in Speech Recognition to convert voice to text. This is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). Recognized text may be sent to Groq's API for math processing (see AI Queries below).

## AI Queries

When you use the AI chat feature, your typed queries are sent directly to Groq's API (api.groq.com) to generate a response. These queries are:
- **Not stored** by Numly
- **Not linked** to your identity
- Subject to [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/)

We recommend not including sensitive personal information in your AI queries.

## Third Party Services

Numly uses the following third-party services:
- **Groq API** — AI math processing. Privacy policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/
- **Apple Speech Recognition** — Voice to text. Privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## No Tracking

Numly does not use:
- Analytics tools (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
- Advertising SDKs
- Tracking pixels or identifiers
- Crash reporting tools that collect personal data

## Children's Privacy

Numly does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.
